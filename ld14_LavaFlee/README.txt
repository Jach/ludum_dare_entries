Yay, Ludum Dare 14 entry.

Goal: get to the end of the "cave"! Escape the Lava Wall of Doom! Rescue the
princess! (Okay she's not there, but still!) Shoot anything in your way!

Controls: z jumps, x attacks, hold z to jump higher, arrows move, p pauses, f
prints out fps.
